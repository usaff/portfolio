Plant Co. Gross Profit Performance Dashboard

Inspiration and Motivation

This dashboard was created to provide in-depth insights into Plant Co.'s gross profit performance across various countries, product types, and time periods. The aim was to enhance decision-making by presenting dynamic and interactive visualizations.

Purpose of the Project

The primary purpose of this project is to analyze year-to-date (YTD) gross profit, compare it to the prior year-to-date (PYTD), and segment profitability by country, product type, and other dimensions.

Key Features

  • Dynamic titles and visuals reflecting selected filters.
  • Comparison of YTD vs. PYTD for Gross Profit, Sales, and Quantity.
  • Profitability segmentation by GP%.

Technologies Used

  • Power BI for data visualization and report generation.
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for dynamic calculations and titles.

Data Source

Data sourced from Mo Chen YouTube Channel

How to Use the Dashboard

Users can interact with the dashboard by selecting metrics (Gross Profit, Sales, or Quantity) and filtering by time period. Dynamic titles and visuals will update accordingly.

Future Improvements

  • Incorporate additional metrics for comprehensive analysis.
  • Improve performance optimization for large data sets.

DAX Formulas and Dynamic Calculations

These DAX formulas play a critical role in the dynamic calculations and title generation for the Plant Co. Gross Profit Performance Dashboard. Here’s a breakdown of each part, highlighting their purpose and functionality:

Dynamic Titles

To enhance user experience, dynamic titles were created to reflect the selected filters, ensuring context-specific insights.

  • Column Chart Title: SELECTEDVALUE(Slc_Values[Values]) & " YTD vs PYTD | Month"
    Displays the selected metric (e.g., Sales, Quantity, or Gross Profit) alongside "YTD vs PYTD" by Month.
    Example Output: "Gross Profit YTD vs PYTD | Month"
  • Report Title: "Plant Co. Performance " & SELECTEDVALUE(Slc_Values[Values]) & " Performance " & SELECTEDVALUE(Dim_Date[Date].[Year])
    Dynamically updates the report title to reflect the selected metric and year.
    Example Output: "Plant Co. Performance Gross Profit Performance 2023"
  • Scatter Chart Title: "Account Profitability segmentation | GP% and " & SELECTEDVALUE(Slc_Values[Values])
    Highlights the relationship between GP% and the selected value.
    Example Output: "Account Profitability segmentation | GP% and Gross Profit"
  • Waterfall Chart Title: SELECTEDVALUE(Slc_Values[Values]) & " YTD vs PYTD | Month - Country - Product"
    Indicates the breakdown by Month, Country, and Product for the chosen metric.
    Example Output: "Gross Profit YTD vs PYTD | Month - Country - Product"

Key Performance Metrics

  • Gross Profit Percentage (GP%): GP% = DIVIDE([Gross Profit], [Sales])
    Calculates the profitability ratio by dividing Gross Profit by Sales.
  • PYTD Gross Profit: PYTD_GrossProfit = CALCULATE([Gross Profit], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)
    Calculates Gross Profit for the corresponding period of the previous year, ensuring seasonality is considered.
  • PYTD Quantity and Sales:
    PYTD_Quantity = CALCULATE([Quantity], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)
    PYTD_Sales = CALCULATE([Sales], SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(Dim_Date[Date]), Dim_Date[InPast] = TRUE)
    Ensures accurate year-over-year comparisons for Quantity and Sales values.
  • Switching Contexts for YTD and PYTD:
    Enables dynamic selection of metrics, enhancing the dashboard's interactivity.
    Example: S_YTD = SWITCH(Selected_Values, "Sales", [YTD_Sales], "Quantity", [YTD_Quantity], "Gross Profit", [YTD_GrossProfit], BLANK())
  • YTD vs PYTD Comparison: YTD vs PVTD = [S_YTD] - [S_PYTD]
    Displays the variance, helping stakeholders understand growth or decline compared to the previous year.

Conclusion

These DAX formulas provide a robust analytical framework, enabling dynamic reporting and insightful visualizations. By using dynamic titles, calculating key metrics, and switching contexts seamlessly, this dashboard supports Plant Co.'s profitability and growth objectives.
